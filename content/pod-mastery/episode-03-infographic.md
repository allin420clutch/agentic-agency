## [SECTION 1 — DESIGN PRINCIPLES: 4 RULES]

**SECTION HEADER:**
## ✅ The 4 POD Design Principles

---

### PRINCIPLE 1 — SIMPLICITY
**Icon:** Single star or diamond shape (minimal, one focal point)
**Rule Text:** One clear focal point. Fewer elements = bigger impact.
**Visual:** Side-by-side: busy design with 7 elements vs. clean design with 1 bold element

---

### PRINCIPLE 2 — BOLD COLORS
**Icon:** Color palette with one bright swatch highlighted
**Rule Text:** High contrast wins. Dark background + bright accent = instant attention.
**Visual:** Same design shown twice — muted palette vs. punchy high-contrast palette

---

### PRINCIPLE 3 — WHITE SPACE
**Icon:** Square with centered dot and outward arrows (breathing room)
**Rule Text:** Empty space isn't wasted space — it's focus space.
**Visual:** Design with elements at the edges vs. same design centered with breathing room

---

### PRINCIPLE 4 — THUMBNAIL READABLE
**Icon:** Smartphone screen with magnified zoom circle
**Rule Text:** Design for 200px first. Scale up second. If it's hard to read small, it's dead.
**Visual:** Zoom flow: tiny thumbnail → expanded mockup → full design detail

## [SECTION 2 — COMMON MISTAKES GRID]

**SECTION HEADER:**
## ❌ 6 Rookie Mistakes That Kill Sales

---

| Mistake | Visual Icon | The Problem | The Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Too Many Elements | Stack of shapes overlapping | Visual noise, impossible to read at a glance | Cut to 1–3 elements max |
| Low Contrast Colors | Pale gray circle | Design disappears on feeds and busy backgrounds | Punch up contrast — use complementing tones |
| Tiny Unreadable Text | Tiny font sample with question mark | Text illegible when printed at actual size | Design text at actual print size, keep it bold |
| Ignoring Product Color | Design on white canvas vs. navy shirt | Colors that pop on white flop on dark shirts | Always mock up on the actual product color |
| Low-Resolution Images | Pixelated image icon | Blurry prints = instant rejection from buyers | Use 300 DPI minimum, 600 DPI for complex art |
| Copying Trending Designs | Copyright strike / takedown icon | Takedowns, saturation, legal risk | Transform inspiration into original work |

**VISUAL LAYOUT:** 2×3 grid of cards, each with icon at top, mistake name bold, brief problem statement, and solution in accent color. Clean iconography style. Consistent card sizing.

## [SECTION 4 — COLOR PSYCHOLOGY MINI-GUIDE]

**SECTION HEADER:**
## 🎨 Color Psychology Cheat Sheet

---

| Color | Psychology | Best Niches | POD Tip |
|---|---|---|---|
| ⚫ **Black** | Power, edge, mystery, authority | Streetwear, edgy quotes, trend niches | Most versatile — works on nearly all shirt colors |
| ⚪ **White** | Clean, minimal, pure, simple | Minimalist designs, wellness, simple quotes | Avoid all-white designs — contrast is still king |
| 🔵 **Navy** | Trust, calm, professionalism | Academic, corporate gifting, older demographics | Lower contrast — pair with bright accent elements |
| 🔴 **Red** | Energy, urgency, passion, action | Fitness, bold statements, attention-demanding designs | Use sparingly — dominant red can feel aggressive |
| 🟡 **Gold/Yellow** | Optimism, warmth, fun, summer | Kids' products, casual niches, summer collections | High visibility — great for outdoor/gear niches |
| 🟢 **Forest Green** | Nature, growth, calm, eco | Wellness, outdoor, eco/nature niches | Earth tones pair well with cream, white, navy |
| 🩷 **Pink** | Playfulness, self-love, style | Girl power, pride themes, gift markets | Often oversaturated — find your specific shade |

## [SECTION 5 — FILE SPECS CHECKLIST]

**SECTION HEADER:**
## 📋 Must-Have File Specs Checklist

---

**Before You Upload to Any POD Partner:**

- [ ] **300 DPI minimum** (600 DPI preferred for complex designs)
- [ ] **CMYK color mode** — not RGB (RGB prints with color shifts)
- [ ] **AI or EPS format** — industry standard for POD production
- [ ] **PNG/JPG only** if your platform specifically allows (last resort)
- [ ] **Bleed area:** 0.125"–0.25" on all sides (print area to edge)
- [ ] **Safe zone:** Critical elements 0.125" inside trim line minimum
- [ ] **Text converted to outlines/paths** — never rely on embedded fonts
- [ ] **Transparent background** for all-over print templates
- [ ] **Downloaded POD partner template** for specific product dimensions
- [ ] **Filename includes:** product type, color, date (e.g., `tshirt_black_v1.ai`)

**VISUAL LAYOUT:** Checklist with bold checkbox icons. Sectioned into two columns: file quality specs on left, file setup/prep on right. Each item with icon indicator. Warning icon next to "Text to outlines" as most commonly forgotten item.

**CALLOUT BOX:**
> 📐 **Always download your POD partner's template** — Printful, Printify, Gelato, and others each have dimension-specific specs. Never assume previous templates still apply when switching partners.

## [SECTION 6 — TESTING BEFORE SCALING]

**SECTION HEADER:**
## 🔬 Test Cheap. Scale What Works.

---

**THE FLOW:**

```
DESIGN IDEA → Small Engagement Ad ($10–20) → Check Click-Through Rate
     ↓
CTR Low? → Revise design or abandon concept
     ↓
CTR High? → Split test two designs (identical targeting)
     ↓
Winner? → Run conversion ads, list product, monitor performance
     ↓
Still Working? → Scale — increase ad budget, expand product variants
```

**VISUAL:**
Simple flowchart in 3 accent colors. Decision diamonds for test points. Clear yes/no branches. Green for "scale," red for "revise/abandon."

**BUDGET GUIDE:**

| Stage | Budget | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Engagement Test | $10–$20 per design | Validate concept gets attention |
| Split Test | $20–$50 per design pair | Prove which design wins |
| Conversion Test | $50–$100 per winning design | Confirm sales potential |
