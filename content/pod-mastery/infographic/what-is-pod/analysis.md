## Main Topic
This content introduces beginners to the Print-on-Demand (POD) business model, explaining what it is, how the step-by-step workflow functions, why it's ideal for beginners, and what pitfalls to avoid.

## Learning Objectives
After viewing this infographic, the viewer should understand:
1. What Print-on-Demand is and how it differs from traditional e-commerce
2. The complete 6-step POD workflow from design to profit
3. Why POD is an ideal low-risk entry point into e-commerce
4. Key platforms and tools involved in the POD ecosystem

## Target Audience
- **Knowledge Level**: Complete beginner
- **Context**: Curious about POD or e-commerce side hustles, looking for low-risk business models
- **Expectations**: Clear, simple explanation of what POD is and how to get started
